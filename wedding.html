Custom instructions let you define shared guidelines that influence how an AI assistant generates code and handles tasks across your workspace. Add instructions once and keep responses consistent.

You can scope instructions globally or to specific files, and you can attach them to individual chats when needed.

Note
Inline editor suggestions may not use these instructions.

Types of instruction files

Markdown-based instruction files are supported. When multiple files exist, they are combined in the chat context.

.github/instructions.md # Project conventions - Use clear naming - Prefer small, focused functions
